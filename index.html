Blog & Research

RF · Automotive
Simulating GPS Coordinates on an Aston Martin Infotainment System
Exploring how easily GPS can be spoofed using a HackRF One and custom TCXO PCB. Covers signal theory, attenuator setup, and the threat model for modern vehicles relying on GPS for autopilot functionality.
02
Physical · RFID
Cloning a Paxton Key Fob with Proxmark3 and Flipper Zero
Research into how keycard entry systems can be exploited. Covers LF/HF card analysis, Proxmark3 workflow, and a real-world test against an apartment entry system with permission.
03
RF · Tesla
Capturing & Replaying RF Charging Signals from Tesla Stations
Captured charging port open signals using HackRF One and Flipper Zero, then successfully replayed them to open a colleague's Tesla (with permission). Explores the security implications and whether other EV makes are similarly exposed.
